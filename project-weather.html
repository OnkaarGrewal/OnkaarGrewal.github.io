Overview

Autonomous weather monitoring system measuring humidity, temperature, wind speed, and rainfall with real-time data logging. Sensor communication protocols implemented in C/C++ on an Arduino-based embedded system using I²C and SPI interfaces.

Mechanical housing designed in SolidWorks — engineered to protect electronics from environmental exposure while maintaining accurate sensor calibration. The housing went through several iterations to solve ventilation vs. waterproofing tradeoffs.

What I Learned

◆
Sensor enclosure designVentilation and thermal isolation determine whether your sensor reads the environment or the inside of your box.
◆
Embedded C/C++ disciplineMemory is tight on ATmega328P — interrupt-driven sensor polling and careful data type choices matter.
◆
Iterative physical designCAD models and 3D prints revealed fit issues invisible on screen — the housing went through three major revisions.
