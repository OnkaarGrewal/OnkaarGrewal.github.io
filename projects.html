Double Pendulum Chaos Simulation

Numerically solved the Lagrangian equations of motion for a double pendulum using 4th-order Runge-Kutta integration in Python/SciPy. Generated phase-space portraits, Poincaré sections, and real-time animations illustrating sensitivity to initial conditions. Extended to compare energy conservation across different step sizes.

Systems diverge visibly from initial condition differences smaller than 0.001° — chaos made tangible.
