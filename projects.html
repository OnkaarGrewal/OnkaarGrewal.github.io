Arduino Spectrometer

Designed and assembled a low-cost visible-light spectrometer using a diffraction grating, a CCD line sensor, and an Arduino Uno microcontroller. Wrote Python scripts for serial data acquisition, wavelength calibration using known emission lines, and spectral plotting. Achieved resolution of approximately 2 nm across 400–700 nm.

Double Pendulum Chaos Simulation

Numerically solved the equations of motion for a double pendulum using 4th-order Runge-Kutta integration in Python/SciPy. Generated phase-space portraits, Poincaré sections, and animations illustrating sensitivity to initial conditions. Extended the model to compare energy conservation across step sizes.
