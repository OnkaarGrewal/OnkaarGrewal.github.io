PID Controller Simulation Suite

Modelled a second-order dynamic system with a full PID controller in Python, implementing proportional, integral, and derivative tuning to analyse transient response. Real-time Matplotlib visualisation compares system behaviour across varying gain parameters. Built as a progressive foundation — inverted pendulum through to spacecraft attitude control.

Same PID loop architecture stabilises satellite orientation, rocket TVC, and re-entry guidance systems — this is the core control loop behind all of it.

Portable Weather Station Node

Autonomous weather monitoring system with a sensor suite measuring humidity, temperature, wind speed, and rainfall with real-time data logging. Sensor communication protocols implemented in C/C++ on an Arduino-based embedded system. Mechanical housing designed and fabricated in SolidWorks to protect electronics from environmental exposure while maintaining accurate sensor calibration.

Designing enclosures that maintain sensor accuracy under environmental stress is the same engineering challenge as aerospace instrument bay design.

Wheelchair-Mounted Assistive Launcher

Led mechanical CAD design of a wheelchair-mounted ball launcher achieving 6 m+ range for a client with arthritis. Full SolidWorks assembly with structural material selection via Granta EduPack. Coordinated technical design integration across a multidisciplinary team in ENGINEER 1P13. Design validated for 50 N+ repeated launch loads with accessible, tool-free wheelchair mounting.

Granta EduPack material selection under cyclic load — the same methodology used in aerospace structural component and airframe bracket design.
